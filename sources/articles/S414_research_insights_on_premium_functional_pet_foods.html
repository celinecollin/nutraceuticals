From Meals to Mixers: The Rise of Functional Add-Ons

The shift toward wellness-focused feeding is also transforming how owners build meals. For manufacturers, this creates momentum for expanding wellness-forward SKUs. For retailers, it signals the importance of guiding owners toward complementary products that support their feeding philosophy.
